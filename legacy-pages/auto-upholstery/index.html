About Autocomfort

Auto Upholstery., founded in 1991, specializes in fully personalized vehicle seats, dashboards, steering wheels, doors and carpets, roofs, convertible soft top roofs, and comprehensive car interior services, glass sun screens. We provide interior modification services for all automobile types, old and new. We are pioneers in the restoration of classic automobile interiors, including the original design. We can supply a broad selection of car seat covers and upholsters made of high-quality fabrics that meet the criteria of individual taste and requirements, as well as the most recent models and designs.

We have our own in-house facilities to provide excellent services using the most up-to-date technologies and procedures. In addition, we have a committed, skilled, and experienced workforce that ensures excellent services that meet the needs of our clients. Our high-quality car seat covers are available in a variety of sizes and may be tailored to fit all of the main automobile models and variations. With each order, a high-quality appearance and client satisfaction is assured.
